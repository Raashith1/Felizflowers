About Us

GardenTulipFlowers - We are the largest retailer of fresh flowers, foliages & ornamental plants in the Middle East growing to evolve as a leader since 2018. With specialised and custom-made bouquets crafted with care by our specialist artists, we give you what no one else can. Your words are expressed florally with care. We have been in this industry for more than two decades, which gives us the thorough knowledge about your likes dislikes and social norms so that you can rest assured when it comes to flowers you gift.
